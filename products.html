Flagship

Triple-Filtered Groundnut Oil

Our groundnut oil is expelled at controlled low temperatures from cleaned, graded kernels, then refined purely by mechanical filtration — never solvents. Three stages of filtration (cloth, plate-and-frame, micron polish) deliver a brilliantly clear, naturally aromatic oil with a high smoke point, ideal for deep frying, tadka and everyday cooking.

Why kitchens choose it

  • Triple filtration: exceptional clarity and shelf stability
  • High smoke point suited to Indian frying and tempering
  • Naturally cholesterol-free, rich nutty aroma
  • No solvent extraction, no blending, no added preservatives
  • Batch-wise lab certificates available to trade buyers

Premium Urad Daal

Whole urad arrives from partner farms and is milled in-house within days: graded, machine de-stoned, conditioned, split and finished to order. The result is uniform ivory splits that soak evenly, grind silky and ferment reliably — the consistency batter units and home kitchens both depend on.

Grades available

  • Urad whole (gota) — cleaned and de-stoned whole grain
  • Urad split with skin (chilka) — split, skin retained
  • Urad split washed (dhuli) — ivory splits, polished or unpolished
