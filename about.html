About Vardhaman Gold

Rooted in soil, raised on standards.

We are an agro-industrial family business with one belief: the food a family trusts most should be the food made with the most care.

Our story

It started with one expeller and a stubborn idea.

Vardhaman Gold began as a single groundnut expeller serving nearby villages. Farmers brought their harvest; families took home oil they could see being pressed. That transparency — watching your own food being made — became our founding standard.

As demand grew, we scaled the machinery but refused to scale away the principle. Every upgrade, from the plate-and-frame presses to the modern daal mill, was chosen to make the product purer, never just cheaper.

Today we operate an integrated facility: groundnut pressing and triple filtration on one line, urad cleaning, de-stoning, splitting and polishing on the other — all under one quality team and one roof.

Our buyers range from neighbourhood kirana stores to regional distributors and food manufacturers, and each gets the same answer to the same question: yes, we'd serve it at our own table.

What we stand for

Three values, zero exceptions.

Purity first

No solvents, no blending, no shortcuts. If a batch fails the lab, it never leaves the plant — whatever the order book says.

Farmer partnership

Long-term sourcing agreements, fair on-time payment and agronomy support across 1,200+ partner acres of groundnut and urad.

Traceability

Every consignment ships with batch records — origin, press date, filtration log and lab results — so audits take minutes, not weeks.

Facility & quality

Industrial scale, kitchen-grade conscience.

The oil line

Cleaned, graded groundnuts are expelled at controlled low temperatures to protect flavour and nutrition. The fresh oil then passes three filtration stages — tight-weave cloth, plate-and-frame press, and a final micron-level polish — before settling tanks and food-grade packing.

The daal line

Whole urad is graded, machine de-stoned, conditioned, split and polished within days of arrival. Optical sorting removes discoloured grain before bagging.

Quality assurance

  • Every batch lab-tested for adulterants before release
  • FSSAI-licensed facility and food-safe packing lines
  • Moisture, FFA and clarity checks at each filtration stage
  • Pest-controlled, humidity-managed grain storage
  • Full batch traceability from farm lot to dispatch
