About Vardhman Gold

Rooted in soil, raised on standards.

From a humble shop in Manasa to an integrated processing facility in Neemuch, our journey has been defined by one simple belief: the food a family trusts most should be the food made with the most care.

Our story

It started with one shop and a stubborn idea.

Our journey began thirty years ago, in 1995, in the trading lanes of Manasa. Starting as a modest wholesale shop, we did not have large machines or expansive networks; we only had our word. Through daily, honest dealings and consistent quality, we slowly earned the most valuable currency in business: the unshakeable trust of our local families.

To serve a growing customer base, we extended our base to Neemuch in 2012. Recognising that modern purity requires modern intervention, we established a state-of-the-art wheat sortex plant. For a decade, that facility set new local benchmarks for grain cleanliness and grading — proof that scale and high standards can walk hand in hand.

Building on our grain processing expertise, we commissioned a dedicated urad daal mill in Neemuch in 2021. By taking complete, in-house control of de-stoning, splitting, conditioning and polishing, we eliminated third-party compromises — establishing ourselves as one of India's most reliable, quality-conscious manufacturers of premium urad daal.

In 2025, we enriched that vision of absolute purity by launching our flagship brand, Vardhman Gold Groundnut Oil — pressed at controlled low temperatures, never refined, and triple-filtered for chemical-free, kitchen-grade purity. In just a few months, it has earned immense love, trust, and a permanent place in household kitchens across the region.

What we stand for

Three values, zero exceptions.

Purity first

No solvents, no blending, no shortcuts. If a batch fails the lab, it never leaves the plant — whatever the order book says.

Farmer partnership

Long-term sourcing agreements, fair on-time payment and agronomy support across 1,200+ partner acres of groundnut and urad.

Traceability

Every consignment ships with batch records — origin, press date, filtration log and lab results — so audits take minutes, not weeks.

Facility & quality

Industrial scale, kitchen-grade conscience.

The oil line

Cleaned, graded groundnuts are expelled at controlled low temperatures to protect flavour and nutrition. The fresh oil then passes three filtration stages — tight-weave cloth, plate-and-frame press, and a final micron-level polish — before settling tanks and food-grade packing.

The daal line

Whole urad is graded, machine de-stoned, conditioned, split and polished within days of arrival. Optical sorting removes discoloured grain before bagging.

Quality assurance

  • Every batch lab-tested for adulterants before release
  • FSSAI-licensed facility and food-safe packing lines
  • Moisture, FFA and clarity checks at each filtration stage
  • Pest-controlled, humidity-managed grain storage
  • Full batch traceability from farm lot to dispatch
